A Note to Sakeena

Since 2023

I know we haven’t really talked in a while, so this isn’t exactly how I imagined sharing how I feel, but I just have to say it. I’ve always seen you as Gwen Stacy, Effortlessly cool, but also genuine and real. You’re someone who just gets people without needing everything spelled out, and even now, I feel like you’d still have my back no matter what. I guess we’re in this strange space between distance and familiarity, where we still understand each other, but don’t really say it out loud. Sometimes we pass by like strangers, and it feels like we’re in our own world, keeping things to ourself. But when it really matters, I know both of us will always show up in a way that counts. And I'd forever be grateful for that.

Author: Jauza Rifa Abdurrafi
